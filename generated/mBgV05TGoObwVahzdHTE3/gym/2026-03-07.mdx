<WorkoutSection title="Deadlift — Top Sets">

  <FormCue label="Setup">
    Bar over mid-foot, hip-width stance. Hinge to grip — push the floor away rather than thinking "pull". Keep lats tight by imagining you're bending the bar around your legs. Brace hard before the bar leaves the floor.
  </FormCue>

  <ExerciseCard
    name="Conventional Deadlift — 135 kg"
    sets={3}
    reps="3"
    rest="4 min"
    rpe={8}
  />

  <FormCue label="PR Attempt Protocol">
    If the 135 kg sets feel solid (RPE ≤ 8), take a 5-minute rest and attempt a single at **142 kg** to push past your current 140 kg PR. Only go for it if your lockout is strong and your lower back feels fresh.
  </FormCue>
